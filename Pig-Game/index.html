About the Game

×

The objective of Pig Dice Game is to score more or equal to the winning points before the opponent does.


GAME RULES:

- The game has 2 players, playing in rounds
- The first player rolls the dice and scores according to the number rolled. That player can roll the dice as much as possible until the high score is reached.
However, the player's turn will end IF:
a. The dice rolls a value of 1. This will also turn the current score to zero.
b. The player decides to save the current score.
-The default winning value is 30 points, but it can be set by the players before the game.
-The person who reaches or surpasses the said winning value, wins the game.

Enjoy!
